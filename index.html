The Recital

A Decade of
Divine Harmony

Carmen Divina: A Decade of Divine Harmony is a thanksgiving recital celebrating 10 years of Carmen Divina’s journey in music and service.

This special evening brings together choral works, instrumental music, and brief moments of reflection that recount the people, stories, and experiences that have shaped the choir over the past decade.

The repertoire draws inspiration from sacred and wedding music that has accompanied many meaningful celebrations throughout the years.

Designed as an intimate and reflective recital, the event invites audiences not only to listen, but to share in a moment of gratitude. Let’s celebrate music, community, and the quiet beauty of harmony.

about carmen divina

The Soul Behind
the Harmony

Carmen Divina is a vocal ensemble founded in 2016 with a shared desire to offer meaningful and reverent music for sacred celebrations, especially weddings. What began as a small group of five individuals has grown into a dedicated community of singers and musicians united by a love for music and a spirit of service.

True to its name — Divine Song — Carmen Divina approaches every celebration not merely as a performance, but as an offering. Known for its light, bright, and resonant choral sound, the ensemble seeks to create moments of warmth, reflection, and beauty through carefully crafted harmonies.

Over the years, Carmen Divina has been entrusted with many meaningful moments at the beginning of many couple’s shared journeys. Guided by sincerity, professionalism, and attentiveness to each ceremony, the ensemble continues to serve with care, humility, and heart.
